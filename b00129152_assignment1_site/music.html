Music

Who do yoy think you are

'Who Do You Think You Are ' - Released in November 1996. The song is heavily influenced by early the early 1990's dance pop and has a disco style beat that resembles the music of the late 1970's The lyrics are about the girls superstar life, and how someone can get trapped in the world of fame. The song Debuted on the UK singles chart at number one and sold 248,800 copies.

Wanna be

'Wanna Be' - this is a upbeat dance-pop song which features Mel and geri rapping. The lyrics, address the value of female friendship over the heterosexual bond, became an iconic symbol of female empowerment and the most emblematic song of the group's girl power. wanna be also hit number 1 on the uk singles chart and sold 7 million copies world wide
